<h1>Groot</h1>
<img src="images/groot.jpg" alt="I am Groot!" id="groot" />
<p>Groot is a tall tree-like character from the Guardians of the Galaxy. This birch-behomoth can only say one phrase: "I am Groot" much to the chagrin of his new found ally, none other than Starlord</p>
<p>Groot's sidekick is Rocket Racoon.</p>
